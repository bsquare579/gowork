## About Gowork

Gowork is a web application that enable enterpreneurer and business owner be discoverable to their customer and also to new customer needing their products and services:

Gowork is accessible, powerful, and provided for small and large scale Business.

## Functionality

1. Accept Gowork to access your location.
2. Get business or comapny nearby within the kilometer of 10KM.
3. View the business or company to get their full description and in details.
